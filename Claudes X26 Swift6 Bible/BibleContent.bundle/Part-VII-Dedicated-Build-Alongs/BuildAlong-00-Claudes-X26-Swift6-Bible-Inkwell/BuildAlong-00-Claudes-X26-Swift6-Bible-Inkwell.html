Reader App: Claude's X26 Swift6 Bible (Inkwell App)

Status: in active development since 2026-04-21. The book's dedicated reader app, under the Inkwell monicker. Runs iPad / iPhone native.

Source tour for the app you are currently reading this chapter in. The Inkwell app is the book's source of truth: the wiki and the eventual EPUB both derive from this app's source structure.

What the Inkwell app is

  • Native SwiftUI reader with a sidebar navigation tree (Parts, Books, Chapters, Pages, Appendices)
  • Ingests BibleContent.bundle directly for offline reading
  • Amber / line-numbered / outline-numbered page layout (matching what you see right now)
  • Search across Lexicon and Book content
  • In-app notes and per-page sharing
  • Under the Hood view exposing the app's own source (same signature trio as the book's primary sample apps)

Source of truth

Every Book, Lexicon Page, and Appendix you read is a file inside this app's source tree. When a page is edited in the Inkwell project, a publisher script pushes updates to the wiki. The EPUB pipeline (for Phase 2 official release via Apple Books and Amazon Kindle) will extract from the same source. The app doesn't consume the book — it is the book's canonical form.

Distribution status

  • Phase 1 (unofficial release): the Inkwell app is already the main reading surface for owners who have it installed. It runs on your iPad right now — which is why you can read this page here.
  • Phase 2 (official release): TBD. Whether Inkwell ships to the App Store or stays a private-testflight reader alongside the commercial book-store edition is still open.
