Part VII — Dedicated Build-Alongs

The uninterrupted build-along track. Each chapter here is a start-to-finish roadmap for a single app, self-contained. You never have to flip back to earlier teaching chapters to assemble the whole picture. Parts I through VI teach concepts using sample-app code as running examples; Part VII shows you how to build each app end-to-end.

Pick the app you want to build, open its chapter, work straight through.
