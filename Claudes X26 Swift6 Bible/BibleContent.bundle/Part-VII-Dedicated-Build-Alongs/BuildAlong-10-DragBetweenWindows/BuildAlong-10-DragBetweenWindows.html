Build-Along 10: DragBetweenWindows

Distribution: TestFlight beta. Single-concept teaching app — dragging items from one window to another across scenes. Does not grow into a full-fledged product on its own, so it ships via TestFlight per the distribution rule.

MVP concept: two windows open side by side (iPad Split View or Mac); drag an item from the left window, drop it into the right. The cross-scene drag IS the teaching point.

What DragBetweenWindows will teach

  • Scenes — WindowGroup hosting multiple active windows, scene phase transitions, per-scene state.
  • Drag & Drop — Transferable, .draggable, .dropDestination, cross-scene payload delivery, handling simultaneous drops.
  • Signature layer: MVP feature + Under the Hood view + About & Send Feedback.
  • Distribution: TestFlight — readers get an install link from the appendix/wiki.

Why TestFlight not App Store

Like TouchAndMove, DragBetweenWindows is a demo of one concept. It doesn't bulk into a real product without bolt-on features that dilute the teaching point. Apple's 2.2 guideline correctly reads it as demo; TestFlight is the right channel.

Current state

  • No Xcode project yet
