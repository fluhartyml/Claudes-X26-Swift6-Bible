Source Tour 19: Tally Matrix Clock

Status: LIVE on the App Store (v2.4.3). Production clock app with WeatherKit integration.

Source tour for Tally Matrix Clock. Anchors the book's WeatherKit attribution discipline lesson.

What Tally Matrix Clock shows at production scale

  • Timer logic and custom clock canvas rendering
  • WeatherKit integration at production scale (see the attribution-variability lesson below)
  • Matrix-style number rendering

The WeatherKit attribution lesson

Tally Matrix Clock was previously approved with its WeatherKit integration, then flagged on a later update for attribution that had previously passed. The lesson that belongs here, not anywhere else: App Store approval is not sticky. Every update has to prove WeatherKit attribution in its own screenshots and copy. A reviewer who didn't notice before can notice now.

  • Source: github.com/fluhartyml/Tally-Matrix-Clock

Shared package dependency

Tally Matrix Clock depends on CryoKit — a curated internal Swift package that also backs CryoTunes Player. See Appendix B: CryoKit for the shared-package source tour.
