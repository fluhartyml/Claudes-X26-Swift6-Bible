Build-Along 13: CloudAndErrors

Distribution: TestFlight beta. Single-concept teaching app — CloudKit sync with proper error-handling plumbing (throws / Result / async). Does not grow into a full-fledged product on its own, so it ships via TestFlight.

MVP concept: a tiny list (grocery list, to-do list, any ordered strings) that syncs across a user's devices via CloudKit Public/Private DB, with every failure mode handled visibly so readers see exactly how to surface sync errors. The app IS the demo.

What CloudAndErrors will teach

  • CloudKit — CKDatabase, record types, subscriptions, push-driven invalidation, optimistic concurrency, iCloud account status.
  • Error handling discipline — throws, Result<Success, Failure>, structured async throws, proper error surfacing in SwiftUI via alerts and inline banners. Partial-failure scenarios (one record saves, another doesn't).
  • Signature layer: MVP + Under the Hood + About & Send Feedback.
  • Distribution: TestFlight.

Why TestFlight not App Store

A "sync a list, show errors" app is pedagogically valuable but doesn't read as a real utility. Readers who want a synced list already have Reminders, Notes, and dozens of third-party apps. The App Store 2.2 bar for "you brought me a demo" would be triggered. TestFlight is the honest home.
