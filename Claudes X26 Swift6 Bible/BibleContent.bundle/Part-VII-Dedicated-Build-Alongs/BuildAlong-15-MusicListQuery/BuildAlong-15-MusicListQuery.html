Build-Along 15: MusicListQuery

Status: concept covered by Source Tours — no separate build-along.

AppleScript / ScriptingBridge querying of the Apple Music library on macOS is already demonstrated at production scale by two Source Tours in this Part. Rather than duplicate the material in a single-concept teaching app, readers are directed to study the production versions.

Where to study this concept instead

  • Source Tour 21 — Cryo Playlist Manager — a Mac app that manipulates the Apple Music library + exports M3U playlists, using AppleScript / ScriptingBridge for the in-place library operations.
  • Source Tour 22 — NightGard Library Commander — a Mac app that cleans Apple Music library metadata in place using AppleScript, with MusicKit for read-only queries. Also shows the sandbox-with-temporary-exception-apple-events pattern.

Why no standalone build-along

A single-concept "query the Music library" teaching app would be a thin demo by itself — it would either ship to TestFlight or need to be bulked up with features that would drift away from the original teaching point. Both Source Tours above already show the concept in a real shipping-product context. Duplicating the material in Part VII would waste the reader's time; pointing at the production tours is cleaner.

Roster note

The slot number 15 stays reserved to preserve the roster's numbering. This entry acts as a signpost, not a build-along. Future revisions may repurpose slot 15 if a genuinely new concept emerges that deserves its own teaching app — but MusicListQuery as originally conceived is retired here.
